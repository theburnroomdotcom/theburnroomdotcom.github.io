Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Burn Room?

The Burn Room is a chef-curated private dining experience. We host intimate, fixed-menu dinners at a single table, serving multi-course tasting menus designed to be experienced slowly.

How many seats are available?

We keep our sessions intimate with limited seats per evening. Exact capacity varies by event, so we recommend booking early to secure your spot.

What about dietary restrictions?

Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements when making your reservation. Our menus typically include seafood, nuts, and dairy. We'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

What should I expect?

Expect a relaxed, unhurried evening. Dress comfortably but elevated. Each course is served with intent, and we encourage you to savor the experience without rush.

What is the pricing?

Pricing varies by menu and session. Details are shared when you reach out to book. Each experience includes the full tasting menu.
