The Story

From Scale to Intention

Chef Keshav grew up in family-run catering and large kitchen environments, where food moved fast and fed many. Later, fine-dining kitchens sharpened that foundation into discipline, timing, and restraint.

Burn Room is where those two worlds meet. Japanese-influenced menus, seasonal produce, and deliberate pacing shape each dinner. The chef hosts, cooks, and serves in close proximity to every guest.

Excellence does not need to be loud.

FAQ

Before You Reserve

How often does Burn Room open?

Burn Room opens once a week with a single curated seating.

How many guests are seated?

Only ten guests are hosted at one table, allowing close chef interaction and paced service.

How do reservations work?

Reservations are confirmed via Instagram DM or phone after checking availability for the upcoming opening.

Can dietary preferences be accommodated?

Yes. Share dietary requirements while reserving so the menu can be planned accordingly.
